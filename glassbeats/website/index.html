Save & Export

High-Quality Synth Kits

Step Accents

Long-press or double-tap any lit step to accent it. That hit plays louder — great for ghost notes, dynamic kicks, and anything that needs to punch.

Randomize tracks or bars with Light, Medium, and Wild intensity. Mutate a bar when it needs variation without starting over.

Tap a track name to preview, randomize, shift, or clear it. Use bar actions to clear, copy, duplicate, vary, accent, or mutate a bar.
